His dedication to learning is reflected in his continuous improvement, whether it’s solving coding challenges, watching tutorials, or learning English grammar alongside coding. Asif's experience is rooted in self-driven learning, online coursework through Codingal, and a strong passion for turning ideas into digital experiences—even at a young age.
